Camping

Looking for an escape? Hesperia Lake is open for camping throughout the year. Open spaces and cool clear air make a perfect setting for spending quality time with the family.

A variety of sites are available at Hesperia Lake to accommodate RV’s and tent campers. All sites have a picnic table, a fire ring, and electricity. Most sites have water

RV sites will accommodate a vehicle up to 40 feet in length. Most sites have water. We do not have a dump station. Restrooms and showers are available at no extra charge. Potable water is located throughout the campground.

Fishing

It's trout season! We will be stocking 1,000 Lb. of rainbow trout from Mt. Lassen Trout Farm every Thursday, unless otherwise posted.

Fishing is allowed on stock days, but the stock cove is roped off. Be sure to arrive early as lines form in the early hours.

No state fishing license is required. Hesperia Lake is a pay-to-fish lake.

Wildlife

Please Don't Touch the Wildlife

We appreciate your help in respecting all of the wonderful wildlife at Hesperia Lake Park. It's especially important during spring/early summer when there are a lot of little ones about the park that can suffer from being chased, touched, picked up, or other traumas.

Please do your part to monitor children in your care as well, and teach them to observe and respect the wildlife from a distance.

Facilities

Picnics

Hesperia Lake Park is a great place to host your next outdoor party, meeting, or celebration! Individual picnic tables are available throughout the park.

Group sites are available for a reasonable fee and reservations must be made in advance. We ask that groups of 25 or more people have reservations.

John Swisher Community Center

Hesperia Recreation and Park District’s John Swisher Community Center, located at Hesperia Lake, offers a unique and innovative setting for your next event.
